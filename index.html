Memorabilia tip — Works best with one item per photo, or a small flat lay of related items. For programs and publications, photograph the cover clearly and include a second shot of the spine and back if condition matters. Include any signatures in frame. The AI assesses event significance, featured players, era, condition, and signatures.
Glassware & ceramics tip — Photograph each piece on a plain light background with good even lighting. Include shots showing the base/maker's mark, any chips or cracks, and pattern details. Multiple angles help — especially for 3D form pieces. The AI identifies maker, pattern, era, and condition including chips, crazing, and repairs.
Electronics & games tip — Include photos showing the model/serial number label, all ports and connectors, any cosmetic damage, and — for games — the cartridge/disc, box, and manual separately if you have them. Completeness (CIB = complete in box) dramatically affects value. The AI assesses model, era, working condition indicators, and completeness.
